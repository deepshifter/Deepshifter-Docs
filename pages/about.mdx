# About

DeepShifter was founded in 2022 by Hayden Frea with the goal of making advancments in fields of Cryptocurrency and Artifical Intelligence.
All too often in the software space are things over complicated and gate-kept to a point where  people cannot access them or use them effectively. We want to make simple tools that people can use to develop great projects!

## Our Values
We want to provide these tools to the open market following the principles of Agape Capitalism.
- Do The Right Thing
- The Golden Rule 
- Lead with Love

Read more about Agape Capitalism here: https://www.agapecapitalism.org/
